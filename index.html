About Manjeet

A Financial Advisor Who Understands Your World

Growing up between two cultures, Manjeet Singh knows firsthand how confusing the American financial system can feel — especially when it comes to protecting your family, planning for retirement, or building a business from scratch.

That's why he built his practice around one simple principle: clear, honest advice that actually makes sense for your life.

With over 7 years at MassMutual Northern California, Manjeet has helped hundreds of families across the South Bay and Peninsula put solid financial plans in place — from first-generation immigrants buying their first life insurance policy, to established business owners planning their exit strategy.

He speaks English, Hindi, and Punjabi fluently, so whether you prefer to talk numbers or dig into the details, you'll always feel understood.
